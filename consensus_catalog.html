Why so many

Same Byzantine Generals problem, different bets

Every protocol below is answering the same question covered in Consensus Mechanisms (PoW, PoS, BFT…): how do mutually distrustful nodes agree on one truth without a referee? PoW, PoS, and BFT voting are the three big families — everything else here is a variation, hybrid, or alternative bet on the same underlying problem.

Keep the trilemma in mind: every entry trades off decentralization, security, and speed. None of the thirteen wins all three — that's why new ones keep getting invented.
